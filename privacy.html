2. Investor-access request form

When you submit the “Request Investor Access” form, we process the data you enter: full name, professional email, organisation/fund, role/title, country, LinkedIn or professional-profile URL, investor type, indicative ticket, potential role in the round, area of interest, decision timeline, requested meeting type and a message, an optional referral source, and the confirmations you give (accuracy of the information and acceptance of this notice).

  • Purpose: to receive, verify and assess your request and to decide on and manage staged, controlled access to investor materials and the Data Room. Submitting the form does not grant access; access is assigned manually after review.
  • Legal basis: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) and steps taken at your request prior to entering into a relationship (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR); our legitimate interest in evaluating and responding to enquiries (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Storage & delivery: your submission is stored in our database (Neon; see §6) and a notification is emailed to us (invest@vitabahn.com) via our email/SMTP provider. An internal routing note we generate for our own assessment is not shared with you.
  • Retention: we keep your request for as long as needed to handle it and any resulting relationship, and delete it at the latest 24 months after the last contact if no business relationship results.
  • Provision: the required fields are necessary to process your request; without them we cannot respond.

3. Investor Data Room account & access logs

If we grant you access, we create an investor account and store: your name, professional email, organisation, role, country and investor type; a securely hashed password (we never store your password in readable form); and your access grant — the assigned access level, NDA status, and any expiry date. Access is per user, time-limited and revocable.

To operate and secure the Data Room and to maintain an audit trail over confidential materials, we keep an access log that records sign-ins (successful and failed), sign-outs, and each document you open or are denied, together with the date and time, your IP address and browser user-agent.

When you open a document, the copy served to you is watermarked with your name, email address and the access time. This personalises each copy so that confidential material can be traced to the account it was released to; the watermarked copy is generated for that request and is not stored. Most documents are additionally view-only and cannot be downloaded.

  • Purpose: controlled, staged disclosure of confidential materials; authentication; security; per-recipient watermarking and an audit trail for the protection of confidential information.
  • Legal basis: steps taken prior to / within a potential investment relationship (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) and our legitimate interest in securing confidential materials, preventing abuse and keeping an audit trail (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Retention: account and access-log records are kept for the duration of the financing process and for a limited period afterwards for security and audit purposes, then deleted or anonymised.

4. Server logs & abuse prevention

To keep the forms available and free of automated abuse, our serverless backend briefly processes your IP address for rate limiting; the public request form additionally requires a client-side proof-of-work challenge. The IP address is used only transiently for this security purpose (short-lived counters) and, separately, is recorded in the Data Room access log described in §3.

Legal basis: our legitimate interest in the security and availability of the service (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

5. Analytics

We use Vercel Web Analytics to understand aggregate usage of the site. It is cookieless and does not track you across other websites. It collects aggregated, non-identifying information such as pages viewed, referrer, approximate region/country (derived from IP), and device/browser type.

Processor: Vercel Inc. Legal basis: our legitimate interest in measuring and improving the site (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). Because the analytics is cookieless and stores no information on your device, it does not rely on consent under § 25 TTDSG.

Some processors above are located in, or may process data in, the United States, so your data may be processed outside the EU/EEA. Where the database is hosted in an EU region, that data stays in the EU; otherwise, and for the other providers, transfers are based on the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses, and we conclude the corresponding data-processing agreements with these providers (Vercel, Neon and Porkbun).

8. Your rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and to object to processing based on legitimate interests. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time with effect for the future. To exercise any right, contact us at invest@vitabahn.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. The authority responsible for the controller is Die Landesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz Niedersachsen (Hannover).

9. Cookies

This site sets no tracking or advertising cookies, and analytics is cookieless. For signed-in investors and founders only, we set a single strictly-necessary session cookie (HttpOnly) so the Data Room and console can keep you authenticated during your session; it contains only a signed session reference, stores no tracking data, and is exempt from consent under § 25(2) TTDSG. It is cleared when you sign out.

10. Changes

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our processing or legal requirements. The “last updated” date above shows the current version.
