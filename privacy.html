2. Investor-materials request form

When you submit the “Request Investor Materials & Data Room Access” form, we process the data you enter: first name, last name, business email, fund/organization, and optionally a ticket range and a message, together with the consent you give.

  • Purpose: to receive, assess and respond to your enquiry and to decide on and manage controlled access to investor materials and the Data Room.
  • Legal basis: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) and steps taken at your request prior to entering into a relationship (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR); our legitimate interest in evaluating and responding to enquiries (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Delivery: your submission is sent to us by email (to invest@vitabahn.com) via our email/SMTP provider.
  • Retention: we keep your enquiry for as long as needed to handle it and any resulting relationship, and delete it at the latest 24 months after the last contact if no business relationship results.
  • Provision: the required fields are necessary to process your request; without them we cannot respond.

3. Server logs & abuse prevention

To keep the form available and free of automated abuse, our serverless backend briefly processes your IP address for rate limiting and requires a client-side proof-of-work challenge. The IP address is used only transiently for this security purpose (short-lived counters) and is not used to build a profile.

Legal basis: our legitimate interest in the security and availability of the service (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

4. Analytics

We use Vercel Web Analytics to understand aggregate usage of the site. It is cookieless and does not track you across other websites. It collects aggregated, non-identifying information such as pages viewed, referrer, approximate region/country (derived from IP), and device/browser type.

Processor: Vercel Inc. Legal basis: our legitimate interest in measuring and improving the site (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). Because the analytics is cookieless and stores no information on your device, it does not rely on consent under § 25 TTDSG.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and to object to processing based on legitimate interests. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time with effect for the future. To exercise any right, contact us at invest@vitabahn.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. The authority responsible for the controller is Die Landesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz Niedersachsen (Hannover).

8. Cookies

This site does not set cookies. Analytics is cookieless, and the site loads no third-party scripts other than the first-party analytics endpoint served from this domain.

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our processing or legal requirements. The “last updated” date above shows the current version.
